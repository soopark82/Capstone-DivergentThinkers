Grandmothers Against Gun Violence

Our project will create an effective tool, graphic, film, and/or visualization that illustrates how every family can help keep children and youth—all family members— safe from gun violence. This project matters because research findings document alarming epidemiology and impacts of firearms on children and youth in the home. Overall, gun violence in the US is on the rise. In 2019 alone, a child or teen was killed with a gun every 2 hours and 36 minutes. Children are more likely to be killed by a gun in their own home than anywhere else. It is estimated that 4.5 million children live in homes with at least one loaded and unlocked firearms.
